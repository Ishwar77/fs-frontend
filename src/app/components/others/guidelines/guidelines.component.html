The Fit Socials Trainer Guidelines

 

1. A platform fee of Rs.2000 is charged during the registration process, and this is nonrefundable.

2. You are expected to provide necessary documents while background verification, falsifying documents could end-up in rejection/termination of your account

3. To conduct sessions online, a trainer is expected to have good internet connectivity and other necessary tools to show himself/herself to the registered audiences, during the sessions.

4. You can request the trainees to turn on the camera, but it is their choice whether or not to reveal themself, during any online sessions.

5. An event created by a trainer is subjected to Approval, by the Administrators.An approval process takes up to three business days.

6. Administrators reserve the right to approve, reject/remove any event.

7. An event must have Subscription options and Batch details specified, if not users cannot signup to such events
